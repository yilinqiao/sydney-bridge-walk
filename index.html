Sydney Harbour Bridge Walk Itinerary

🌤️ Saturday Walk Plan · Arrive at Town Hall by 3:30 PM

🕘 Timeline

  • 11:00 AM – Arrive at Milsons Point (Recommended: T1 Line to Milsons Point Station)
  • 11:15 AM – Walk to Lavender Bay via the edge trail of Bradfield Park
  • 11:30 AM – Explore Wendy’s Secret Garden 🌿 (Approx. 30 min)
  • 12:00 PM – Return to Milsons Point, prepare to cross the bridge
  • 12:15 PM – Start walking across the Sydney Harbour Bridge 🚶‍♀️
  • 12:45 PM – Arrive at Pylon Lookout 🔭 (Highly recommended – entry ~$19)
  • 1:45 PM – Continue walking toward Circular Quay via George Street
  • 2:00 PM – Relaxed lunch or coffee ☕ (Suggestions: Bar Cycle, Guylian Café, or nearby bakeries)
  • 2:45 PM – Head to Town Hall via Metro or walk along Pitt Street
  • 3:30 PM – Arrive at Town Hall ✅

📌 Highlights

  • 🌉 Walk across the iconic Sydney Harbour Bridge (approx. 1.5km)
  • 🔭 Enjoy panoramic views at Pylon Lookout
  • 🌿 Discover the tranquil charm of Lavender Bay and Wendy’s Secret Garden
  • ☕ Scenic break at Circular Quay

🎒 What to Bring

  • 🧢 Hat & sunscreen (expected sunny weather)
  • 🥤 Water bottle (most of the walk is outdoors)
  • 📸 Phone or camera
  • 👟 Comfortable walking shoes
