🌉 Sydney Harbour Bridge Walk Itinerary

🕘 Timeline

  • 11:00 AM – Arrive at Milsons Point (Recommended: T1 Line to Milsons Point Station)
    到达 Milsons Point 火车站 （建议乘坐T1火车线）
  • 11:15am – Walk to Lavender Bay via Bradfield Park
    漫步至 Lavender Bay（穿越 Bradfield 公园）
  • Optional: Visit Kirribilli Markets (Usually open on Saturday or Sunday — check dates before visit. Located between Milsons Point Station and Bradfield Park)
    （可选）游览 Kirribilli 市集（通常开放时间为周六或周日，建议提前查好日期。市集位于 Milsons Point 火车站和 Bradfield 公园之间）
  • 11:30am – Rest and enjoy the view at Lavender Bay
    在 Lavender Bay 稍作停留，欣赏海湾风景
  • 12:00pm – Explore Wendy’s Secret Garden 🌿 (~30 mins)
    探索 Wendy 的秘密花园（约 30 分钟）
  • 12:30pm – Return to Milsons Point Elevator (Northern Side) and prepare to cross the bridge
    返回 Milsons Point，准备跨桥
  • 12:45pm – Start walking across Sydney Harbour Bridge 🚤
    开始步行跨越悉尼港大桥 🚤
  • 1:15pm – Arrive at Pylon Lookout on the bridge ($29.90 entry) 🌍
    抵达 Pylon Lookout（位于大桥上，门票 $29.90）
  • 2:00pm – Continue to Circular Quay via The Rocks and stroll to George St
    继续前往 Circular Quay，并沿 George St 散步

📌 Highlights

  • 🌉 Sydney Harbour Bridge Walk – 1.5km across the iconic bridge
    漫步悉尼港大桥，全线 1.5 公里
  • 🔭 Pylon Lookout – panoramic views from the bridge tower
    Pylon 观景台，窗口观看海湾景色
  • 🌿 Lavender Bay – peaceful greenery and secret gardens
    Lavender Bay 美丽海湾、秘密花园
  • 📢 Kirribilli Markets – optional stop for local crafts & food
    Kirribilli 市集，展售本地手工艺品和美食

🎒 What to Bring

  • 🧂 Hat & sunscreen (for sunny weather)
    帽子和防晒霜（如今天晴）
  • 🥤 Water bottle
    水壶
  • 📸 Camera or phone
    相机或手机
  • 👟 Comfortable walking shoes
    舒适的步行鞋
